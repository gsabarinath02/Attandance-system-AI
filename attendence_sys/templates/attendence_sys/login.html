I hope this message finds you well. I would like to introduce myself as G Sabarinath, I am currently working on a comprehensive AI-based exam proctoring system, and I would like to share with you a module I have been developing.

The module I have created is an AI Attendance System that utilizes face verification to mark the attendance of students. This system grants faculty members the permission to take attendance, add students, and modify student details. Additionally, faculty members can search for a student's attendance using a multiparameter search feature, specifying the student ID, date of attendance, and period of attendance.

To access the system, faculty members are provided with credentials by the superuser, who has access to the entire database. Only the superuser can update a student's attendance. For the development of this web application, I utilized the Django web framework. To implement the face recognition functionality, I incorporated OpenCV and the face_recognition API. The Face Recognizer component is capable of detecting multiple faces simultaneously and recording their attendance in the database.
